Skills:  Design and Branding


I designed the Mocha Moms Loudoun, & Fairfax website. Mocha Moms, Loudoun & Fairfax is an information website where prospective members learn more information on how to become a member of a local chapter of Mocha Moms. Mocha Moms is a non-profit organization. I included social media icons to link the user to other ways to stay engaged with the chapter. The site is fully responsive.
